"I received a job offer mid-course, and stepping into the working world felt like crossing the threshold from the enchanting realm of Harry Potter into the ordinary realm of Muggles. However, to my surprise, the subjects I had learned in my studies were not only relevant but even more cutting-edge in the company I joined. It was as if I had brought a piece of the magical world with me, and I couldn't help but feel that I had truly received every penny's worth of my education."

Intrigued and with gold coins my parents left me, I decided to dip my toes in the magical waters by signing up for the free intro course. And now, since completing the course, I have successfully made the transition. It feels like I've entered a parallel universe where lines of code hold the power to create wonders. The junior year was my very own Hogwarts, where I discovered my true potential and unlocked the door to a world of endless possibilities. I am forever grateful for the transformative experience that has allowed me to wield the magic of programming and embark on a new journey in the realm of technology."
